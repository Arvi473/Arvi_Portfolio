006 : Water Leakage Detection System

I have build this Water Leakage Detection System, using hardware components [RaspberryPi, GrovePi+, Adafruit RGB 16X2 LCD with Keypad kit, Grove Water Sensor, LED, Buzzer, NoIR Camera] & Software [Linux, Python programming, Blynk App].

"The water leakage detection system can promptly use to prevent the leak in pipelines and to recognize the breaks and alter it by this structure. This project targets decreasing leaks which have various advantages for both the organization similarly as the purchaser, including more essential relentless quality through a powerful water structure and besides saving water for sometime later. Regardless, the leakage of water is unavoidable as a result of specific conditions, for instance, manufacturing defects, corrosion, ageing of pipes, overflow of bath tubs, leaky taps and flood floors. The project Water Leakage Detection System contains three Pi plates they are: Raspberry Pi, Grove Pi+ and Adafruit RGB Positive 16X2 LCD+Keypad Kit. The use of Grove Pi+ board and the Grove water sensor plays a major role in developing the whole Water Leakage Detection System. Raspberry Pi is the basic source for this system to function where, it supplies 5V power to the whole system and the micro SD card holds the entire data of the system. After the execution of code we can see the output displayed in the terminal window of raspberry pi as 1 and 0, 1 indicates that the grove water sensor is in a dry state and 0 is when the sensor detects the water or moisture. Additionally, LED and Buzzer is added to the Grove Pi+ board for the better understanding of the system. When the sensor detects water, the LED will glow and the buzzer sounds. The Blynk app is connected to the system and mobile through which, the owner gets the Notifications and Emails through the wireless network (Lan cable or WiFi – Dongel) that connected to the Raspberry Pi, when there is water or the moisture content on the surface of the grove water sensor. The Adafruit RGB Positive 16x2 LCD+keypad kit for Raspberry Pi permitting a red or green backlight illumination showing visually the status of the water sensor. When the grove pi water sensor is in a dry state, the LCD will display the message as “System is Super!” with the green color backlight and when the grove water sensor is in a wet state, then automatically the backlight of the LCD display turns to red color with the message as “Water is Leaking”. In case the running system hangs up for no good reason, an active built-in watch dog would start up the structure by utilizing the contents of the water leakage detection system."

Aravind Santhosh Nagaraju
